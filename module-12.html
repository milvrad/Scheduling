Module 12 • Final checks

🚀 Publishing, Quality Assurance, and Completion

Publish only after reconciliation, specialty checks, downstream systems, and approval steps are complete.

WhatWhyWhat to useHow to use itSelf-check
Module progress:

Learning objectives

  • Use the pre-publication checklist before clicking Publish.
  • Identify common errors and how to prevent them.
  • Publish weekly and weekend schedules with the correct settings.
  • Use self-check scenarios to confirm readiness for independent scheduling tasks.

Why this matters

  • Publishing makes the schedule visible as the plan people rely on. Errors can trigger confusion, missed coverage, unfair assignments, and downstream rework.
  • Quality assurance is the scheduler’s final safety net before the schedule becomes operational.

What to use

  • QGenda Bulk > Publish for weekly schedule publication.
  • QGenda Bulk > 2 Days Publishing for weekends.
  • Pre-publication checklist for days off, Evening STAT Grid, call/weekend, IR seating, home/admin/tumor board notes, Fluoro Outpatient tasks, WebExchange consistency, paid/extra work, and publish settings.
  • Dr. Gor/Dr. Kricun review step before weekly publication.
  • Self-check scenarios in the Completion Checklist.

How to use it

  1. Confirm the Days Off Grid is reconciled against QGenda and pending requests.
  2. Confirm the Evening STAT Grid is updated to avoid duplicate or unfair evening assignments.
  3. Resolve over-limit days-off issues or escalate them before publication.
  4. Confirm hard-to-fill call/weekend days are covered and avoid call before vacation or directly onto weekend workers unless unavoidable and approved.
  5. Confirm IR radiologists are seated for the full week and two sitting radiologists are assigned every evening.
  6. Confirm home/admin/tumor board notes, Fluoro Outpatient tasks, Neuro fluoro balance, Peds/NM/NHWK WebExchange consistency, and paid/extra work documentation.
  7. For weekly publishing: Bulk > Publish, set Monday-Sunday dates, check all Staff, check all Tasks, uncheck all LBL, No Evening, Total Eve Counter, and NHK Vac, then approve and publish.
  8. For weekend publishing: Bulk > 2 Days Publishing, check all Staff, select the required weekend/call/site task groups, check all under Weekend Schedule, publish, and verify.
  9. After publication, re-open QGenda and verify the schedule displays correctly.

Common errors to prevent

RiskPrevention
Old document usedCheck current scheduling notes, current quarter folder, QGenda, and newest file date.
QGenda updated but WebExchange not updatedUse a post-change systems checklist for NHWK/Peds/NM call.
Swap submitted but tracking not changedAlways complete Admin Track/Untrack steps after swap.
Home/remote not documentedAdd request-date and location/home note to QGenda.
Paid shift missing from payrollWatch for $ symbols, sold shifts, extra work, and volunteer coverage.
PET volume overstatedSeparate PET brain studies and exclude them from total count.
New radiologist scheduled too soonVerify start date, onboarding, system access, contract, and restrictions.
Evening sitting coverage shortVerify two sitting in department every night.
